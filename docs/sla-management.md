# Service Level Agreement (SLA) Management

## Purpose
To define expected response and resolution times for IT services.

---

## SLA Categories
- Incident response
- Service requests
- Access provisioning
- System availability

---

## SLA Targets (Example)
- Critical incidents: 1-hour response
- High priority: 4-hour response
- Medium priority: 1 business day
- Low priority: 3 business days

---

## SLA Monitoring
- Ticket system reporting
- Performance dashboards
- Monthly reviews

---

## Breach Handling
- Root cause analysis
- Corrective action
- Stakeholder communication

---

## Outcome
Improved service reliability and user satisfaction.
